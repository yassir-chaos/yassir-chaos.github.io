What we do

Our mission at Norm is to drive external interest and optimize internal effectiveness for our clients.
Focussing on the interplay between data, processes, and content, we provide transformative strategic intelligence to growing companies.

We offer two interlinked strands of expertise:


Training

Beginning with a free, one-to-one consultancy session, we map your processes to reduce your time to market, determine which technologies and innovations would benefit you, and discover how the data you generate from business-as-usual can lead to practical insights. From there, we propose actionable solutions for drawing your current operational model closer to your target one.

Infrastructure

Beyond operations, it’s important to drive engagement and interest among your existing client base, and to find points of connection with future customers. As part of our stakeholder engagement offer, we deliver a comprehensive suite of technical documents, reports, and presentations.
