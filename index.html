What we do

Our goal is to help ideas become real. We leverage our deep backgrounds across hardware and software to validate a concept, and then prepare for production.

We can step in at any point in your development journey:


Training

If you want to skill up your internal development teams, we can help through on-site or remote training programs. Ranging from higher level concepts like database design, to best practices, all the way to crash-courses on specific program languages and their nuances.

Infrastructure

Your developers are ready to go, but you want a development infrastructure that can help them build robust, well tested products. We can help you set up your own development infrastructure, including self-owned AI agents, source control like Gitlabs, coding environments, docker repositories and more.

Build

For when you don't have the bandwidth or resources to prove out an idea, or develop a new product, we can help you. From FPGA, to PCB all the way up to software apps, we can help you anywhere that you need it.
